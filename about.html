About MSSO Industries

Powering Industries with Smart Engineering Solutions Efficient, Reliable, and Scalable Manufacturing Support.

MSSO Industries is a premier engineering & industrial goods trading company based in Electronic City, Bangalore. We deliver complete solutions for sheet metal component and assembly needs — from prototype development to high-volume production, industrial machining, heat treatment, surface finishing, and door-to-door logistics. Our commitment to precision, cost efficiency, and on-time delivery makes us a trusted partner across energy, automotive, construction, and infrastructure sectors.
